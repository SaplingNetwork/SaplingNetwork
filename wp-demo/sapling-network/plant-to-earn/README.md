# Plant to Earn

The Plant 2 Earn (P2E) feature is a core part of our project, enabling users to earn tokens by planting saplings. This system connects blockchain technology with environmental goals, providing a clear and effective way to support sustainability while earning rewards.

Users can plant saplings either physically or through our application. Token rewards are granted based on various criteria related to the planted saplings. During sapling purchase or staking transactions, a minimal transaction fee will be charged, and users can choose, prior to completing the transaction, whether these fees will be directed towards sapling planting initiatives or renewable energy investments.
