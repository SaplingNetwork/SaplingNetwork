# Community Members

#### How to be a Senior Member?

Anyone can be a member of the Sapling Community, but there are certain requirements for participating in activities and holding management authority. These are:

Users who are in the top 50 of the monthly tree planting leaderboard can apply to become senior members. The first 10 members on the leaderboard are automatically promoted to senior member level without voting, only through pre-screening and suitability tests. The application will be reviewed by the management team, and pre-selections will be made before being presented to the voting of both senior community members and regular community members. The purpose of the voting is to give the Sapling Community members a say in the decisions made and to create a fair election environment. After the voting, suitability tests will be conducted for the members who received the most votes. The elections will be completed when a total of 25 members successfully pass the suitability tests.\
\
The main purpose of this process is to ensure that governance rights are held only by individuals who genuinely embody ecological ideals.
