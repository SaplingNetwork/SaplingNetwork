# Community Rewards

Users can plant trees via the application either through a donation method to partner organizations, or through senior members. Senior members can accept the task of planting the closest tree to their area via the application. There are multiple tasks involved in the tree planting process, including planting the tree, entering detailed data and uploading photos via the restor.eco platform after the planting process.

The process of earning token rewards is similar to the example above. The details of the reward system are as follows:



Senior members will be able to earn tokens for each tree they plant, based on a predetermined rate. For example, for each tree planted, community members could earn 1 $SAPN.

In the task section of the application, senior members can accept the tree planting task that is closest and most suitable to them. The minimum number of trees that can be accepted at once is 10.

The task section clearly states where and how the trees will be obtained. Members are responsible for planting the trees, as shown in the video tutorial.

To earn extra rewards after the tree planting process, the planted area must be selected through the restor.eco platform, the number of trees planted must be specified, and photos of the environment before and after planting, as well as the planted trees, must be added. Additionally, the restor.eco link must be added through the application.

After all checks have been made by the community manager, rewards will be credited to the user's account.



* Tokens earned by community members can be used to planting trees.
* Tokens earned by community members can be used in the project's ecosystem, for example, to participate in decision-making processes, access certain features and services.
* Tokens earned by community members can also be traded on cryptocurrency exchanges, giving them an additional way to monetize their efforts.
