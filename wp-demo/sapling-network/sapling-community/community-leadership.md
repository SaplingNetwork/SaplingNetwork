# Community Leadership

The community leaderboard is a key feature of the Sapling Community. The aim of the leaderboard is to recognize the efforts of community members in reducing carbon emissions. The leaderboard will provide a transparent and real-time update of the community's collective impact, serving as a source of inspiration for members to get involved.

Each community member will earn points for various actions, such as planting trees, participating in community events, and making a positive impact on the environment. The points earned by each member will be used to rank them on the leaderboard. The leaderboard will be publicly available, giving members the opportunity to share their progress with others and to see how they stack up against other community members.

The leaderboard will provide a gamification aspect to the project, making it more fun and engaging for members to participate. It will also help to create a sense of community and camaraderie as members work towards a common goal of reducing carbon emissions. The leaderboard will be updated in real-time, allowing members to see their progress as they make an impact.

The leaderboard is an essential component of the Sapling Community, serving as a source of inspiration, motivation, and recognition for community members. It will help to create a positive and supportive community atmosphere, encouraging members to take action and make a positive impact on the environment.
