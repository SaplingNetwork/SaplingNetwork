# 5. Tokenomics

#### 5.1 Token Overview

* Token Name: Sapling Network
* Ticker: SAPN
* Blockchain: Solana
* Maximum Supply: 100,000,000 SAPN

#### 5.2 Token Allocation

* Community & P2E Rewards: 35%
* Ecosystem Development: 20%
* Team & Advisors: 15% (vesting applied)
* Treasury & Sustainability Fund: 15%
* Liquidity & Market Making: 10%
* Strategic Partnerships: 5%

#### 5.3 Vesting Schedule

* Team & Advisors: 12-month cliff, 36-month linear vesting
* Ecosystem & Treasury allocations released based on governance approval

#### 5.4 Utility & Demand Drivers

SAPN tokens are used for:

* Governance participation and voting
* Plant-to-Earn rewards
* Staking and network security
* Access to premium ecosystem features

#### 6.5 Inflation Control

SAPN uses a capped supply with emission reductions tied to ecosystem maturity. As network activity increases, emission rates decrease, creating long-term scarcity.
