# 8. Plant-to-Earn & Verification

Plant-to-Earn (P2E) lets users generate real environmental impact — and earn from it.

#### 8.1 How trees get planted

* **Partner planting (Phase 1):** Users purchase sapling packages in the app; plantings are executed by established reforestation partners. Pricing is USD-indexed and paid in SAPN.
* **Community planting (Phase 2+):** Verified **Senior Members** accept planting tasks near their location (minimum batch: 10 trees), follow the documented planting procedure, and submit evidence.

#### 8.2 Verification — no proof, no tree

Every planting is verified before it counts:

1. Planting area is registered on **restor.eco** with location data
2. Before/after photographs and tree counts are submitted through the app
3. A community manager reviews the evidence
4. The batch is anchored on-chain: batch ID, tree count, GPS region, certificate hash
5. The planter receives their reward; the buyer receives a **compressed NFT certificate** per tree

#### 8.3 Earning

* Senior Members earn a fixed SAPN reward per verified tree planted
* Every tree cNFT generates a small ongoing SAPN yield for its owner ("tree yield"), halving with the emission epochs
* Rewards are funded exclusively from the 40M community pool — never from new supply
