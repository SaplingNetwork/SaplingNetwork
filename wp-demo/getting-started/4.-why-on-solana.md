# 4. Why on Solana?

Sapling Network is built on Solana due to its unmatched energy efficiency.

* Average energy per transaction:
  * Bitcoin: 1943 kWh
  * Ethereum: 192.45 kWh
  * Solana: 0.00051 kWh

A single Bitcoin transaction consumes as much energy as approximately four million Solana transactions, making Solana the optimal foundation for an environmentally responsible blockchain ecosystem.
