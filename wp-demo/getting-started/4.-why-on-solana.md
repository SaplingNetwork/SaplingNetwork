# 4. Why on Solana?

Sapling Network is built on Solana for four reasons:

1. **Energy efficiency.** At \~0.0005 kWh per transaction and total network emissions measured in the low thousands of tonnes of CO₂e per year, Solana is among the most climate-efficient L1s in production — the natural home for an environmental protocol.
2. **A reachable flagship goal.** Because Solana's total footprint is small, offsetting it entirely is achievable. Sapling Network's headline milestone is to plant enough verified trees to make **the Solana ecosystem the first carbon-negative major blockchain** (see Section 12).
3. **Cost structure.** Sub-cent transaction fees make micro-rewards, per-tree NFT certificates, and Round-Up donations economically viable.
4. **Ecosystem maturity.** Deep DeFi liquidity, compressed NFTs for near-zero-cost certificates, and battle-tested tooling.
