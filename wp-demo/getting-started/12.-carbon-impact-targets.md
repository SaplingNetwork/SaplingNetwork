# 12. Carbon Impact Targets

Honesty first: offsetting the entire crypto industry (\~65–90 Mt CO₂e/yr) would require billions of mature trees. That is our generation-scale vision, not a short-term promise. Our targets are staged, funded, and verifiable:

| Phase       | Period    | Cumulative trees                                                                   | Annual absorption at maturity                                                                                             |
| ----------- | --------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First Grove | 2026      | 1,000                                                                              | \~22 t CO₂                                                                                                                |
| Phase 1     | 2027      | 25,000                                                                             | \~550 t                                                                                                                   |
| Phase 2     | 2028–2029 | 250,000                                                                            | \~5,500 t — **exceeds Solana's estimated annual network emissions: the first carbon-negative major blockchain ecosystem** |
| Phase 3     | 2030–2032 | 2,500,000                                                                          | \~55,000 t                                                                                                                |
| Phase 4     | 2033–2036 | 10,000,000                                                                         | \~220,000 t                                                                                                               |
| Vision      | 2036+     | Billion Tree Coalition — cross-project alliance targeting industry-wide neutrality | —                                                                                                                         |

Progress is tracked live on the transparency dashboard; no tree is counted without verification evidence.

#### 12.1 Measurement methodology

Our absorption figures are deliberately conservative:

* **Per-tree absorption:** We use **22 kg CO₂ per mature tree per year** as the baseline — a mid-range figure consistent with commonly cited estimates for mixed-species reforestation (actual rates vary by species, climate, and soil, roughly 10–40 kg/yr). Where planting partners provide species- and region-specific data, we use their certified figures instead.
* **Maturity curve:** Trees do not absorb at full capacity from day one. Our dashboard reports two numbers separately: **projected absorption at maturity** (the figures in the table above) and **current-year estimated absorption**, which ramps up over \~10 years following planting. We never present projected capacity as current impact.
* **Mortality buffer:** A fraction of planted saplings does not survive. We apply a **20% mortality discount** to all absorption projections, and partner replanting guarantees are tracked per batch. Targets in the table count _verified, surviving_ trees.

#### 12.2 Verification pipeline

A tree enters our counters only after completing the full evidence chain described in Section 8.2: registration of the planting area on restor.eco → geotagged before/after photographs and tree counts → review by a community manager → on-chain anchoring of the batch record (batch ID, tree count, GPS region, certificate hash). Partner plantings additionally require the partner's planting certificate. Anyone can audit any batch from the dashboard down to its on-chain record and photographic evidence.

#### 12.3 The "carbon-negative Solana" benchmark

For the Phase 2 milestone we benchmark against **independent third-party estimates of the Solana network's annual operational emissions** (e.g. Crypto Carbon Ratings Institute reports commissioned by the Solana Foundation, most recent available edition). The milestone is met when our verified trees' **current-year estimated absorption** (not projected maturity capacity) exceeds that benchmark for a full calendar year — measured conservatively, using the mortality-discounted figures. We publish the benchmark source and calculation openly, and update it as new network emission reports are released.

#### 12.4 What we do not claim

We do not claim to offset Bitcoin, the whole crypto industry, or emissions we cannot measure. We do not sell or double-count carbon credits: trees funded through Sapling Network are retired to our public ledger and are not resold into voluntary carbon markets. If a claim cannot be verified from our public data, we will not make it.
